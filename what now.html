What Now?

After reviewing and reading everything about Haiti you may be wondering, “what can I do now?” One way that you can help is by spreading awareness and letting people know what’s truly going on because not everyone knows the truth about Haiti. It took a massive earthquake to hit Haiti just to have people talk about the country, but that shouldn’t be the case. This country is beautiful and is filled with beautiful people and culture, but has been taken advantage of for many years now. This disadvantage that has taken place has put the country in a downward spiral, but we still encourage each other to change and help each other. Another thing that can be done is sending money directly to the county that will benefit the people. This one is a tough one because money seems to get lost very easily in Haiti, so finding the right organization and people to send your money to can be tricky. This will require you to do extensive research and judgment that who and wherever you’re sending your money to will be used to help the country. In the end, Haiti will overcome these setbacks and prosper as I believe it can!
