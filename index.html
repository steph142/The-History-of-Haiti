When you type in Google the words “Haiti” you are shown a collection of images of Haiti being in distraught and chaos within the island. If you’re not familiar with Haiti and Haitians, you would believe that this is a dangerous island and that the people there need to leave. Although the country does have its ups and downs just like other countries, there is still sunshine and deep culture left that keeps its people going. Many may wonder, “how did it get to this point?” Well, no need to wonder as I take you on a journey throughout Haiti!

Haiti is a beautiful country filled with beautiful people and culture that will amaze you everytime you learn and hear about it. The country so far holds about 11.4 million people and seems as if it will continue to grow. Many people may wonder why people continue to have kids within the county? The answer is because many of them know that not all their children will make it and so they have more than needed, also for the possibility of having a breadwinner in the family.

Many other third world countries do this as well. Haiti has suffered a lot over the years and information about their mistreatment needs to be told which is why this website was created! Dive into the culture of Haiti and fill your brain with all the knowledge you are going to learn today!
