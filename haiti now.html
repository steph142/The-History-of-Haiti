Haiti Now

Haiti now is in a state of need for repair. Haiti has given all of its resources and support to other countries just to be tossed to the side. The latest breaking news that has happened in Haiti was the assassination of the former president and the country is still in shame because of it. Haiti needs people who actually want to repair the country because too many people have offered their help just to steal it right back. Our government needs refinement and better structure and we also need to crack down on where the country's money has gone. That money that was given can be very beneficial to the country and allow it to have a head start in repairing itself in order to bring in more money to the country. Haiti has a long way to go, but these are some headstart advice that they can take.
