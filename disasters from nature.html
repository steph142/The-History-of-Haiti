Disasters From Nature

Haiti is no stranger to natural disasters as the country has faced many, one of the most notable disasters being the world breaking news of the earthquake on January 12, 2010. The earthquake had a high magnitude of 7.0and left the capital, Port-au-Prince in shambles with destructive buildings and killed about 220,000 people who were there at the time. When many people think of Haiti this incident is what comes to mind because of its wide coverage and the horrible destruction that it caused the country. I am Haitian and I could recall the day this event took place and how distraught my family was as they worried about our other family members and everyone else who was in Haiti at the time. This event was surprising to me because this was the first time I heard people talking about Haiti because Haiti before then was “unknown” to many people, especially here in America. Haiti received lots of money ($6 billion) and aid to recover from this disaster, but many wonder what happened to the money? On the next page, we’ll look more into it.
